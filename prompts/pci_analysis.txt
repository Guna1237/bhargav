Score each PCI dimension 0-100 based on the actual content of responses:

- group_alignment (25%): How consistently do team members understand the project goals and what success means?
  Low score = different interpretations of the problem. High score = shared understanding.

- priority_alignment (25%): How aligned are they on what to build first and what matters most?
  Low score = conflicting priorities. High score = clear shared priority order.

- role_clarity (20%): How clearly has each person thought through their specific role?
  Low score = vague or overlapping contributions. High score = distinct, concrete ownership.

- skill_coverage (15%): Does the collective skill set cover what the project actually requires?
  Low score = skill gaps or duplicate skills. High score = full coverage with no critical gaps.

- tool_cohesion (15%): How aligned are communication and coordination preferences?
  Low score = conflicting tool preferences. High score = consensus on workflow.
